Snap, Upload, Act: Upcycle Items Around the House with Fun Ideas!

Upcycle Today!

Did you know that more than half of the waste in landfills comes from households, and around 25% of it is placed in the wrong bin? This means we are sending reusable items to waste sites, further contributing to global warming.

However, we can prevent this from happening with EcoCycle, a web app that uses a deep-learning image classification model to identify items in your home and provide creative ideas for upcycling them, helping reduce household waste.

How Does It Work?

1. Upload an image of your item


2. Let an AI-based deep learning image classification model give YOU tips to repurpose your waste!
